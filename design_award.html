The multimedia interactive interface of pregnancy 懷孕互動介面設計

Award 2010

Life breeding is a love journey, but it needs to overcome many hurdles. Our purpose is to let pregnant women feel sense of security, joyful of the newborn life. We originally provide an interactive multimedia platform with professional and humanly interface to our potential mothers. Our aim is to establish good communication between doctors and patients through the media.

獲得 「The 7th International Competition NAGOYA DESIGN DO! 2010 Division Prize」 日本名古屋設計大賞

畢業製作 「懷孕互動介面」是從準媽媽的角度出發，以動畫和互動的方式帶給媽媽們從產檢、常見疾病、孕期 10 個月胎兒的變化怎麼影響到媽媽、胎兒出生...等，讓爸爸和媽媽可以一起透過互動了解懷孕這件事，並期許為女性不受重視、甚至會因為懷孕而備受歧視的國家更重視女性懷孕這件事。

負責項目：文案 / 概念發想 / 使用流程 / 設計 / 動畫
